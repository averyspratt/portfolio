Campus Cactus

The Problem:
We aimed to design a single solution to two problems. The primary focus was to make the transition to college a little easier for incoming freshmen with the added goal of building a stronger sense of community between students, universities, and local businesses.

The Solution:
 Campus Cactus! For the past two years, Campus Cactus has held events welcoming incoming freshmen to Tufts University with the best dorm decor you can get - a cactus! I am proud to say that my internship project provided Tufts University students access to easy-to-care-for dorm plants, but the success of these events didn't stop there. As if the extensive scientific research proving the benefits of house plants wasn't enough, Campus Cactus built a strong relationship with Tufts, Loyal Supply Co, and a Massachu setts Plant Nursery and gave students an introduction to the wonderful community of Somerville they are now a part of.
